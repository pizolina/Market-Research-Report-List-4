<p><p>Fruits Drying Machine market includes two main types: Atmospheric Drying Machine and Vacuum Drying Machine. Atmospheric Drying Machine operates at normal atmospheric pressure and uses hot air to dehydrate fruits. This method is commonly used for drying fruits like apples and bananas. Vacuum Drying Machine, on the other hand, operates under low pressure conditions, removing moisture from fruits more efficiently. This method is suitable for delicate fruits like berries and grapes. Both types of machines play a crucial role in the fruit drying industry.</p></p>
