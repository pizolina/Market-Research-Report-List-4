<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Dental Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-etching Adhesive</li><li>Selective-etching Adhesive</li><li>Total-etching Adhesive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental adhesives are classified into three types: self-etching adhesive, selective-etching adhesive, and total-etching adhesive. Self-etching adhesive does not require the use of a separate etching process, making it faster and easier to use. Selective-etching adhesive selectively etches the enamel, while total-etching adhesive etches both enamel and dentin surfaces for better bonding. Each type of adhesive offers unique advantages and is used in different dental procedures based on the specific requirements of the treatment.</p></p>
