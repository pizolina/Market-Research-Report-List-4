<p><p>Enterprise Data Management encompasses two main market types: On-Premise and Cloud-based solutions. In contrast, Cloud-based solutions offer data management services through the internet, allowing for scalability, accessibility, and cost-effectiveness. Both options cater to different organizational needs, with On-Premise solutions favored by industries requiring strict data control, while Cloud-based solutions appeal to businesses seeking flexibility and scalability.</p></p>
