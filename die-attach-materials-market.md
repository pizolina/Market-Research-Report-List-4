<p><strong>The Die-Attach Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die Attach Paste</li><li>Die Attach Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Die-attach materials are used in the assembly of semiconductor devices. Two common types are die attach paste and die attach wire. Die attach paste is a material that is commonly used for surface mount applications, providing strong adhesion between the die and substrate. Die attach wire, on the other hand, is a fine wire made of materials like gold or aluminum that is used to physically connect the die to the substrate. Both materials play a crucial role in ensuring the reliability and performance of electronic devices.</p></p>
