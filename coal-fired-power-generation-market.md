<p><p>In coal-fired power generation, two main market types exist: Pulverized Coal System and Cyclone Furnaces. Pulverized Coal System involves grinding coal into powder, then burning it in a boiler to produce steam for electricity generation. Cyclone Furnaces, on the other hand, use cyclone separators to burn coal particles, which increases efficiency and reduces emissions. Both systems are widely used in power plants around the world, with Pulverized Coal System being more common due to its established technology and efficiency.</p></p>
