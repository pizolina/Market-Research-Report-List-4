Three axis cranes have articulation at the base, mid-section, and tip of the boom, offering greater flexibility in movement. Four axis cranes feature an additional articulation point at the base, allowing for even more precise positioning. Other market types include specialized cranes with unique configurations or features designed for specific industries or tasks. These variations cater to different needs in terms of reach, stability, and maneuverability.</p></p>
