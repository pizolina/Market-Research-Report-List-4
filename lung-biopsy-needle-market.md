<p><p>The Lung Biopsy Needle market consists of two main types: Outer Needle and Inner Core Needle. Outer Needle biopsy needles are used to puncture the skin and reach the target area in the lung for sample collection. Inner Core Needle biopsy needles have an additional inner needle that collects tissue samples once the outer needle is positioned. Both types play a crucial role in the accurate diagnosis of lung conditions and help in guiding treatment decisions for patients.</p></p>
