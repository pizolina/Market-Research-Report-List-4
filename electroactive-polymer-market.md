<p><p>Electroactive polymer market includes different types such as Conductive Plastics, Inherently Conductive Polymers (ICPs), and Inherently Dissipative Polymers (IDPs). Conductive Plastics are polymers that have conductive fillers added to increase their electrical conductivity. Inherently Conductive Polymers (ICPs) are polymers that have intrinsic conductive properties due to their structure. Inherently Dissipative Polymers (IDPs) are polymers that are designed to have static dissipative properties, making them suitable for applications where static electricity needs to be controlled.</p></p>
