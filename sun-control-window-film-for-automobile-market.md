<p><p>Sun control window film for automobiles is available in three main types: clear (non-reflective), dyed (non-reflective), and vacuum coated (reflective). Clear window film allows for maximum visibility while still blocking harmful UV rays. Dyed window film provides some level of heat and glare reduction without being reflective. Vacuum coated window film is highly reflective, offering superior heat and glare reduction. Each type caters to different preferences and needs of consumers looking to protect their vehicles from the sun's harsh rays.</p></p>
