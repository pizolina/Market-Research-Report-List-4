<p><p>Straight market barrier gates are the most common type, moving straight up and down to block or allow access. Crank market barrier gates, on the other hand, have a mechanism that allows them to be manually cranked open or closed. Both types of barrier gates are controlled through wired systems, providing security and control over vehicular access. The straight market gates are more automated and efficient, while the crank market gates offer a manual backup option in case of power outages or system malfunctions.</p></p>
