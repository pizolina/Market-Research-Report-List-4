<p><p>The nuclear ionizer market can be classified into three main types: AC ionizers, pulsed DC ionizers, and steady-state DC ionizers. AC ionizers generate ions by applying alternating current, while pulsed DC ionizers produce ions through pulses of direct current. Steady-state DC ionizers provide a continuous flow of ions using direct current. These ionizers are used in various industries such as nuclear power plants, research facilities, and medical institutions to help remove static charges from surfaces and improve air quality.</p></p>
