<p><p>Pepsinogen Test kits are available in different market types such as Immunochromatography and Chemiluminescence Immunoassay. Immunochromatography involves the use of a solid phase membrane to detect pepsinogen levels in a sample, providing quick and simple results. On the other hand, Chemiluminescence Immunoassay utilizes a chemical reaction to produce light, allowing for precise and sensitive detection of pepsinogen. Both market types offer efficient and accurate ways to measure pepsinogen levels for diagnostic purposes.</p></p>
