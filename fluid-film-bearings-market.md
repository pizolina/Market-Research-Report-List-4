<p><ul><li>Hydrostatic Bearings</li><li>Hydrodynamic Bearings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fluid film bearings are primarily classified into two types: hydrostatic bearings and hydrodynamic bearings. Hydrostatic bearings utilize an external pump to supply fluid under pressure to create a film between the surfaces, while hydrodynamic bearings rely on relative motion between the shaft and bearing to generate a fluid film. These types of bearings are commonly used in various applications such as rotating machinery, automotive engines, and industrial equipment, providing smooth and efficient operation by reducing friction and wear.</p></p>
