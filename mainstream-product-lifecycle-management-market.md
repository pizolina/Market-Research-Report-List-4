CPDM focuses on collaboration and data management throughout the product development process. MCAD involves designing and drafting of mechanical components using computer software. S&A includes tools for simulating and analyzing product performance. DM encompasses digital tools for manufacturing processes planning and optimization. These market types cater to different aspects of product development within the PLM landscape.</p></p>
