<p><p>The dental handpiece market consists of three main types: air-driven handpiece, electric handpiece, and hybrid air-electric handpiece. Air-driven handpieces are powered by compressed air and are cost-effective but have limited power. Electric handpieces are powered by a motor, offering higher torque and precision but come at a higher cost. Hybrid air-electric handpieces combine the advantages of both, providing power and precision while remaining cost-effective. Each type caters to different needs and preferences within the dental industry.</p></p>
