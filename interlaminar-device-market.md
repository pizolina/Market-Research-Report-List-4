<p><p>Interlaminar devices are used to treat spinal stenosis by providing stability and relieving pressure on the nerves. Interspinous distraction devices are inserted between the spinous processes to maintain spacing and reduce compression on the nerves. The Coflex device is a type of interlaminar device that is implanted in the back to provide support and prevent excessive movement. These devices help alleviate symptoms such as pain, numbness, and weakness by promoting proper spinal alignment and reducing pressure on the spinal cord.</p></p>
