<p><ul><li>PCBN</li><li>PCD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The superhard cutting tool market can be divided into two main types: PCBN (polycrystalline cubic boron nitride) and PCD (polycrystalline diamond). PCBN tools are known for their superior hardness and thermal conductivity, making them ideal for cutting hard materials like steel and cast iron. On the other hand, PCD tools offer high wear resistance and low friction when cutting non-ferrous materials like aluminum and copper. Both types of superhard cutting tools are essential in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and machining.</p></p>
