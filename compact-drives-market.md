<p><p>Compact drives are categorized into three main market types based on voltage levels - low-voltage, medium-voltage, and high-voltage markets. Low-voltage compact drives typically operate at voltages below 1000 volts and are commonly used in small to medium-sized industrial applications. Medium-voltage compact drives operate at voltages between 1000 and 10,000 volts, and are used in larger industrial applications such as mining and oil & gas. High-voltage compact drives operate at voltages above 10,000 volts and are utilized in heavy-duty industrial applications like power generation and large-scale manufacturing.</p></p>
