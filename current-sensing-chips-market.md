<p><strong>The Current Sensing Chips Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open-loop Current Sensor</li><li>Closed loop Current Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Open-loop current sensors are non-contact devices that measure current by detecting the magnetic field generated by the current flowing through a conductor. They are cost-effective and suitable for high-current applications. Closed-loop current sensors, on the other hand, provide a precise measurement of current by comparing the input and output signals. They offer higher accuracy and better linearity compared to open-loop sensors. Both types are widely used in various industries such as automotive, industrial, and consumer electronics for monitoring and controlling current flow.</p></p>
