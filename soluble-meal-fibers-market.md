<p><p>Soluble meal fibers market can be categorized into different types such as inulin, polydextrose, pectin, and others. Inulin is extracted from chicory roots and helps in promoting gut health and improving digestive functions. Polydextrose is a synthetic fiber that acts as a bulking agent in food products. Pectin is a natural fiber found in fruits and is known for its ability to lower cholesterol levels. Other types of soluble fibers include guar gum, psyllium husk, and resistant starch, which also offer various health benefits such as aiding in weight management and regulating blood sugar levels.</p></p>
