<p><p>Encrypted Hard Disk Drives come in two main types: Hard Disk Drive FDE and Enclosed Hard Disk Drive FDE. Hard Disk Drive FDE refers to a drive that has built-in encryption capabilities that protect data on the drive itself. Enclosed Hard Disk Drive FDE, on the other hand, is a physical external drive that provides encryption for data stored on the drive. Both types offer enhanced security for sensitive information and are popular choices for individuals and businesses looking to protect their data from unauthorized access.</p></p>
