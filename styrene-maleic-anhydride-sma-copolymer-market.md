<p><p>Styrene Maleic Anhydride (SMA) Copolymer comes in two main types - A-SMA and R-SMA. A-SMA is an amorphous copolymer with good thermal stability and excellent transparency, making it suitable for use in packaging materials, coatings, adhesives, and medical devices. On the other hand, R-SMA is a rubbery copolymer with high impact resistance and flexibility, often used in engineering plastics, automotive parts, and consumer goods. Both types offer unique properties and cater to different applications in the market.</p></p>
