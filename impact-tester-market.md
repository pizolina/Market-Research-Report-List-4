<p><p>Impact Tester Market consists of various types such as Charpy Test and Izod Test, which are commonly used for evaluating the impact resistance of materials. Charpy Test involves striking a notched specimen with a pendulum, while Izod Test measures the energy needed to break a specimen. Other types of impact testers include drop weight testers, torsion testers, and spring hammer testers. These testers play a crucial role in industries like manufacturing, construction, and automotive, where assessing material toughness is essential for ensuring product quality and safety.</p></p>
