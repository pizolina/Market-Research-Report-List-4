<p><p>Small beer market types include Limit Fermentation and Dealcoholization Method. This method allows for a beer that is lighter and more sessionable for consumers looking to moderate their alcohol intake. Dealcoholization Method, on the other hand, involves removing alcohol from a fully fermented beer to create a non-alcoholic or low-alcohol alternative. This process allows for consumers to enjoy the taste of beer without the effects of alcohol.</p></p>
