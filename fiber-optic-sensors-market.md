<p><strong>The Fiber-Optic Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Intrinsic Fiber-Optic Sensors</li><li>Extrinsic Fiber-Optic Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Intrinsic fiber-optic sensors are those where the sensing element is built into the fiber itself, allowing for direct interaction with the measurand. Extrinsic fiber-optic sensors, on the other hand, have a separate sensing element that interacts with the fiber to detect changes in the measurand. Both types of sensors offer high sensitivity, immunity to electromagnetic interference, and the ability to operate in harsh environments, making them ideal for a wide range of applications in industries such as aerospace, oil and gas, and healthcare.</p></p>
