<p><p>Feed Grade Urea is available in two main types on the market: Granule and Powder. Granule feed grade urea is produced by compacting urea into small granules, making it easier to handle and distribute. It is often preferred for its uniform size and slow-release properties. On the other hand, Powder feed grade urea is finely ground urea that dissolves quickly, making it suitable for instant mixing and feeding applications. Both types of urea have their own advantages and are widely used in the agricultural industry.</p></p>
