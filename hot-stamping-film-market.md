<p><p>Hot stamping film is available in various thicknesses to cater to different applications. The most common types in the market are 12μm, 16μm, 18μm, and 20μm. The 12μm film is commonly used for delicate designs or materials that require a light touch. The 16μm film is suitable for general purpose hot stamping applications. The 18μm film provides increased durability and is ideal for heavier substrates. The 20μm film is the thickest option, offering maximum strength and resilience for tough stamping jobs.</p></p>
