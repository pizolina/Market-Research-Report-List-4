<p><p>Inclinometer ICs are available in two major market types: Surface Mount and Through Hole. Surface Mount Inclinometer ICs are designed to be mounted directly onto the surface of a printed circuit board, offering a compact and space-saving solution. On the other hand, Through Hole Inclinometer ICs are mounted by inserting the leads through holes in the PCB and soldering them on the other side, providing a more secure and rugged connection. Both types cater to different application requirements and PCB designs in the electronics industry.</p></p>
