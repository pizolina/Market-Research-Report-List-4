<p><p>Cardiac output monitoring equipment market offers three types of monitoring options - Invasive, Minimally Invasive, and Non-Invasive. Invasive monitoring involves the insertion of a catheter into the blood vessels to directly measure cardiac output. Minimally Invasive methods utilize advanced technologies to measure cardiac output with less intrusion. Non-Invasive monitoring includes methods such as echocardiography or bioimpedance to measure cardiac output without the need for invasive procedures. Each type of monitoring provides healthcare professionals with valuable insights into a patient's cardiac function for better management of heart conditions.</p></p>
