<p><p>Infrared food dryers can be classified into two main types: continuous type and discontinuous type. Continuous type infrared food dryers are designed for continuous processing of large quantities of food, allowing for high-speed and efficient drying. Discontinuous type infrared food dryers, on the other hand, are better suited for small-scale or batch processing, making them more suitable for smaller operations or research purposes. Both types of dryers use infrared technology to quickly and evenly dry food products while preserving their nutritional content and flavor.</p></p>
