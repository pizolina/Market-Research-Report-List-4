<p><p>Implantable cardiac defibrillators are devices used to monitor and treat abnormal heart rhythms. The market is divided into two main types: single chamber and dual chamber devices. Single chamber devices have one lead that monitors and delivers shocks to the heart, while dual chamber devices have two leads that monitor and deliver shocks to the upper and lower chambers of the heart. Both types of devices are important in treating life-threatening heart conditions and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
