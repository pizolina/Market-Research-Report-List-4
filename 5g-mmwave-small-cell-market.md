<p><p>The 5G mmWave small cell market can be divided into two main types: integrated small cell and distributed small cell. Integrated small cell refers to a single unit that combines the mmWave radio, antenna, and baseband processing in one enclosure, making it easy to deploy and manage. On the other hand, distributed small cell involves separate components for the radio and antenna, allowing for more flexibility in placement and coverage. Both types of small cells play a crucial role in expanding 5G coverage and capacity in urban areas.</p></p>
