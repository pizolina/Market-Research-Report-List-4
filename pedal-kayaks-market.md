<p><p>Pedal kayaks come in two main market types: push pedal and rotational pedal. Rotational pedal kayaks, on the other hand, require the user to pedal in a circular motion, similar to riding a bicycle. Both types provide a hands-free paddling experience, allowing users to cover more distance with less effort and enabling them to fish, take photos, or simply relax while on the water.</p></p>
