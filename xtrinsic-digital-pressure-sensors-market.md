<p><p>Xtrinsic Digital Pressure Sensors have three main types in the market: Relative Digital Pressure Sensors, Absolute Digital Pressure Sensors, and Differential Digital Pressure Sensors. </p><p>- Relative Digital Pressure Sensors measure pressure relative to atmospheric pressure, commonly used in altimeters and barometers.</p><p>- Absolute Digital Pressure Sensors measure pressure relative to a perfect vacuum, ideal for high-altitude applications.</p><p>- Differential Digital Pressure Sensors measure the difference in pressure between two points, commonly used in flow measurement and HVAC systems. Each type serves a specific purpose based on the pressure reference point needed for accurate measurements.</p></p>
