<p><p>Boiler blowdown valves market types are categorized by blowdown type and product type. By blowdown type, they can be classified as intermittent or continuous blowdown valves. Intermittent blowdown valves release small amounts of water periodically while continuous blowdown valves release a continuous stream of water. By product type, they can be further divided into top and bottom blowdown valves. Top blowdown valves are located at the top of the boiler for easy access, while bottom blowdown valves are located at the bottom of the boiler for effective removal of sediments.</p></p>
