<p><p>Mineral screening machines are used in the mining industry to separate valuable minerals from waste material. There are two main types of mineral screening machines: Fixed Screen and Rolling Screen. Fixed screen machines have a stationary frame with a screen panel that the material passes through, while rolling screen machines have a rotating drum that allows the material to be screened as it moves along the drum. Both types of machines are essential for efficient mineral processing in the mining industry.</p></p>
