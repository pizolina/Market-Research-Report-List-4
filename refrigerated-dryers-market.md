<p><p>Refrigerated dryers are used for removing moisture from compressed air in various industrial applications. There are two main types of refrigerated dryers: Intermittent Type and Continuous Type. Intermittent Type dryers are designed to operate on a cycle basis, cycling on and off to remove moisture from the compressed air. Continuous Type dryers, on the other hand, operate continuously to provide a constant flow of dried air. Both types are used based on specific application requirements and can effectively remove moisture to ensure the quality of the compressed air.</p></p>
