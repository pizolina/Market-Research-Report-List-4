<p><p>Pneumatic diaphragm valves can be categorized into three main types: normally closed type, normally open type, and reciprocating type. Normally closed type valves are closed when there is no air pressure, while normally open type valves are open in the absence of air pressure. Reciprocating type valves have a back-and-forth movement mechanism for regulating flow. Each type has its specific application in various industries based on the desired flow control requirements.</p></p>
