<p><p>Ultra High-Performance Liquid Chromatography (UHPLC) detectors are crucial components in separating and analyzing compounds in a sample. The main types of detectors include UV-Visible detectors, which measure absorption of light by compounds, Refractive Index detectors, which detect changes in refractive index caused by eluting compounds, Evaporative Light Scattering Detectors (ELSD), which are sensitive to non-UV absorbing compounds, Fluorescence Detectors, which emit light when excited by specific wavelengths, and other detectors such as electrochemical or conductivity detectors that offer different detection capabilities for specific compounds.</p></p>
