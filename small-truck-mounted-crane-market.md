<p><p>Small truck mounted cranes come in different types to suit various lifting needs. Folding boom cranes have a pivoting arm that can be folded for easy transportation and storage. Telescopic boom cranes have extendable arms that can reach farther distances. Truss boom cranes use a lattice structure for increased strength and stability. Each type has its own unique advantages and is used in different applications based on the specific requirements for lifting heavy loads in construction, maintenance, and other industries.</p></p>
