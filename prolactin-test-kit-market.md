<p><ul><li>Time-resolved Immunofluorescence</li><li>Chemiluminescence Immunoassay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Prolactin Test Kit Market types include Time-resolved Immunofluorescence and Chemiluminescence Immunoassay. Time-resolved Immunofluorescence uses specific antibodies labeled with a fluorescent marker to detect prolactin levels, offering high sensitivity and accuracy. On the other hand, Chemiluminescence Immunoassay utilizes a light-emitting reaction to measure prolactin levels, providing quick results and high precision. Both methods are commonly used in medical laboratories for diagnosing conditions related to prolactin levels such as infertility, irregular menstruation, and pituitary disorders.</p></p>
