<p><p>Asphalt distributors can be classified into two main types: drag and self-propelled. Drag asphalt distributors are attached to a vehicle and pulled along a road to distribute asphalt evenly. On the other hand, self-propelled asphalt distributors have their own engine and can move independently to spray asphalt on the road surface. Both types of distributors play a crucial role in efficiently and accurately spreading asphalt for road construction and maintenance projects.</p></p>
