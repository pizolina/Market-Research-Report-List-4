<p><p>Portal crane market includes various types such as Gantry Cranes and Half-Gantry Cranes. Gantry cranes are characterized by their horizontal beam supported by upright legs, allowing them to move materials along a track. Half-Gantry cranes have one leg mounted to a runway beam while the other end rides on wheels along the ground. Both types are commonly used for heavy lifting in industrial settings, construction sites, and ports. They offer efficient and versatile solutions for transporting goods and materials.</p></p>
