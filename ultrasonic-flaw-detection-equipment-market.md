<p><p>Ultrasonic flaw detection equipment market includes three main types: Conventional Ultrasonic Flaw Detection Equipment, Phased Array Ultrasonic Flaw Detection Equipment, and TOFD Ultrasonic Flaw Detection Equipment. </p><p>Conventional equipment is the most basic form, using a single-element transducer to detect flaws. Phased Array equipment uses multiple elements to create a detailed image of the flaw, offering improved detection capabilities. TOFD equipment uses time-of-flight diffraction to provide accurate sizing and characterization of flaws. Each type has its own advantages and applications in the field of flaw detection.</p></p>
