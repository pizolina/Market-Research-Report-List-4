<p><p>Over-the-counter mouthwashes are divided into two main market types: alcoholic and non-alcoholic. Alcoholic mouthwashes contain ingredients like ethanol that can help kill bacteria and freshen breath, but may cause a burning sensation in some users. Non-alcoholic mouthwashes, on the other hand, do not contain ethanol and are often preferred by individuals with sensitivity to alcohol or dry mouth. Both types are widely available in stores and offer a convenient option for maintaining oral hygiene outside of professional dental care.</p></p>
