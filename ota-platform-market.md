<p><ul><li>Firmware Over-the-air (FOTA)</li><li>Software Over-the-air (SOTA)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>OTA platforms cater to both Firmware Over-the-Air (FOTA) and Software Over-the-Air (SOTA) markets. FOTA involves remotely updating a device's firmware to enhance performance, security, or add new features. On the other hand, SOTA focuses on updating software applications on devices to improve functionality or fix bugs. Both FOTA and SOTA market types play a crucial role in ensuring devices are up-to-date, secure, and optimized, saving time and resources for both businesses and end-users.</p></p>
