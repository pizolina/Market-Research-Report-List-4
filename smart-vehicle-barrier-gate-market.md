<p><p>Smart Vehicle Barrier Gate Market Types include Straight and Crank barriers. </p><p>Straight barriers are stationary and typically used in areas where vehicles approach at a straight angle. They are popular in parking lots and toll booths. </p><p>On the other hand, Crank barriers are retractable and can be adjusted to different angles, making them versatile for various entry points. They are commonly used in areas with limited space or irregular vehicle approach angles. Both types of barriers enhance security and control vehicle access efficiently.</p></p>
