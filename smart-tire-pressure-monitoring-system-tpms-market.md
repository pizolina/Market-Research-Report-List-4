<p><ul><li>Indirect Tire Pressure Monitoring System</li><li>Direct Tire Pressure Monitoring System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smart Tire Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS) is divided into two types: Indirect Tire Pressure Monitoring System and Direct Tire Pressure Monitoring System. Indirect TPMS uses wheel speed sensors to detect changes in tire pressure, while Direct TPMS utilizes pressure sensors within each tire to provide real-time pressure readings. Indirect TPMS is generally less expensive but less accurate, while Direct TPMS offers more precise monitoring but at a higher cost. Both systems help improve safety, fuel efficiency, and overall tire performance.</p></p>
