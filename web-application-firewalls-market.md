<p><p>Web Application Firewalls (WAFs) can be divided into two main market types: Cloud-Based and On-Premises. Cloud-Based WAFs are hosted and managed off-site by a third-party provider, offering scalability and ease of deployment. On the other hand, On-Premises WAFs are installed and managed within the organization's own infrastructure, providing greater control and customization. Both market types aim to protect web applications from a variety of threats such as DDoS attacks, SQL injections, and cross-site scripting. Overall, the choice between Cloud-Based and On-Premises WAFs depends on factors such as budget, resources, and security requirements.</p></p>
