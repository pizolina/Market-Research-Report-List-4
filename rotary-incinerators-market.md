<p><ul><li>Counter Current Rotary Incinerators</li><li>Co-Current Rotary Incinerators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rotary incinerators are classified into two main types based on the direction of gas flow: Counter Current Rotary Incinerators and Co-Current Rotary Incinerators. In Counter Current Rotary Incinerators, waste material and combustion gases flow in opposite directions, maximizing efficiency by ensuring thorough combustion. On the other hand, Co-Current Rotary Incinerators involve waste material and combustion gases flowing in the same direction. Each type has its own advantages and applications, catering to different needs in waste management and disposal.</p></p>
