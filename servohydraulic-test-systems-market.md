<p><p>Servohydraulic Test Systems cater to two main types of testing markets: Dynamic Cyclic Testing and Static Testing. Dynamic Cyclic Testing involves subjecting materials or components to repeated cycles of loading and unloading to simulate real-life conditions and evaluate fatigue strength and durability. On the other hand, Static Testing involves applying a constant load to assess the material's strength, stiffness, and deformation characteristics. These two markets represent different testing methods to ensure the quality and reliability of components and materials in various industries.</p></p>
