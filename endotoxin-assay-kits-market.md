<p><p>Endotoxin Assay Kits market types include Gel Clot Endotoxin Test, Chromogenic Endotoxin Test, and Turbidimetric Endotoxin Test. Gel Clot test involves gelation reaction to detect endotoxins, Chromogenic test uses chromogenic substrate to produce colored compounds indicating endotoxin presence, while Turbidimetric test utilizes turbidity changes to measure endotoxin levels. Each type offers different advantages and applications based on specificity, sensitivity, and ease of use, catering to various endotoxin testing needs in industries such as pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and healthcare.</p></p>
