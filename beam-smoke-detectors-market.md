<p><strong>The Beam Smoke Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reflective</li><li>Projected</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Beam smoke detectors are categorized into two main types: reflective beam detectors and projected beam detectors.</p><p>Reflective beam detectors consist of a transmitter and receiver that are mounted facing each other, with the beam of light reflecting off a target to detect smoke particles. These detectors are ideal for large open spaces and high ceilings.</p><p>Projected beam detectors use a transmitter and receiver that are separated by a distance and create a beam of light that detects smoke particles. These detectors are commonly used in atriums and warehouses.</p><p>The global beam smoke detector market is expected to see significant growth in the coming years, driven by increasing safety regulations and the growing demand for advanced fire detection systems in commercial and industrial applications.</p></p>
