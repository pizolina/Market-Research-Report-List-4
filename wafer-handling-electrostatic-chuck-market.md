<p><p>Wafer Handling Electrostatic Chuck is a device used in semiconductor manufacturing to securely hold wafers during processing. There are two main types of Electrostatic Chucks: Coulomb Type and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type. Coulomb Type Chuck uses electric charge to attract and hold the wafer in place, while JR Type Chuck utilizes a combination of Coulomb forces and Van der Waals forces for wafer handling. Both types offer high precision and stability, making them essential tools for efficient semiconductor production.</p></p>
