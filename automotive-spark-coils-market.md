<p><p>Automotive spark coils are classified into two main types - single-spark and multi-spark coils. Single-spark coils produce a single spark per ignition cycle, while multi-spark coils generate multiple sparks per ignition cycle. Single-spark coils are typically simpler in design and more cost-effective, while multi-spark coils are known for providing improved engine performance, combustion efficiency, and reduced emissions. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various automotive applications based on specific requirements and performance goals.</p></p>
