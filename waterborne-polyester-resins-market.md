<p><ul><li>Saturated Waterborne Polyester Resins</li><li>Not Saturated Waterborne Polyester Resins</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Waterborne polyester resins are classified into two types based on saturation levels: saturated waterborne polyester resins and not saturated waterborne polyester resins. Saturated waterborne polyester resins have all available sites occupied by chemical bonding, making them more stable and resistant to environmental conditions. On the other hand, not saturated waterborne polyester resins have some available sites for further chemical reactions, offering potential for customization and modification. Both types cater to different application needs and provide unique advantages in various industries.</p></p>
