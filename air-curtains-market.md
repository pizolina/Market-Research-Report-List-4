<p><p>Air curtains are a type of equipment used to create a barrier of air to separate two different environments while allowing easy passage for people and objects. The market for air curtains can be divided into two main types: re-circulating air curtains and non-re-circulating air curtains. Re-circulating air curtains utilize a fan to recirculate the air within a space, while non-re-circulating air curtains draw in fresh air from outside. Both types serve the same purpose of maintaining temperature control and preventing contaminants from entering a space.</p></p>
