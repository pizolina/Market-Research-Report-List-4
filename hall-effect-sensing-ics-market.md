<p><p>Digital Output Hall Effect Sensing ICs are integrated circuits that provide a digital output signal based on the presence or absence of a magnetic field. On the other hand, Analog Output Hall Effect Sensing ICs provide a continuous analog output signal that varies based on the strength of the magnetic field. These ICs are used in applications where a more precise measurement of the magnetic field is required.</p></p>
