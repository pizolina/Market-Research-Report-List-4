Single screw laboratory extruders are widely used for research and development purposes in industries such as plastics, food, and pharmaceuticals. On the other hand, twin screw laboratory extruders are preferred for their high torque capabilities and ability to handle more complex formulations. They are commonly used in industries requiring higher mixing and dispersion capabilities.</p></p>
