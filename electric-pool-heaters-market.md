<p><p>Electric pool heaters come in two main types: constant temperature furnaces and heat pump markets. Constant temperature furnaces work by maintaining a consistent temperature in the pool water throughout the day, providing a reliable source of warmth. Heat pump markets, on the other hand, utilize the surrounding air to heat the pool water, making them more energy-efficient and cost-effective in the long run. Both types of electric pool heaters offer different benefits and cater to different consumer needs in the market.</p></p>
