<p><p>Digital Airfield Management Systems (DAMS) are available in two market types: On-premises and Cloud-based. On-premises DAMS are installed and maintained on the organization's servers, giving them full control and customization capabilities but requiring high upfront costs. In contrast, Cloud-based DAMS are hosted on the provider's servers, offering lower costs, scalability, and accessibility from anywhere with an internet connection.
