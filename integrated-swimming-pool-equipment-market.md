<p><p>Integrated swimming pool equipment market consists of two main types: wall-mounted and underground. Wall-mounted equipment is installed above ground, usually on the pool deck, and includes filters, pumps, and heaters. Underground equipment, on the other hand, is installed below ground level, typically in a designated equipment room, and includes similar components as wall-mounted systems. Both types offer various benefits depending on the pool size, design, and budget requirements, providing flexibility and efficiency in maintaining a properly functioning swimming pool system.</p></p>
