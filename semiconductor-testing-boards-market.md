<p><p>Semiconductor testing boards refer to specialized equipment used to test semiconductor devices. There are three main types of testing boards in the market: Probe Cards, Load Boards, and Burn-in Boards. </p><p>Probe Cards are used to enable electrical testing of semiconductor devices by making contact with the device's terminals. Load Boards are used for testing the functionality of semiconductor devices under different load conditions. Burn-in Boards are used to subject semiconductor devices to accelerated aging conditions to ensure their reliability over time. Each type of testing board serves a specific purpose in the semiconductor testing process.</p></p>
