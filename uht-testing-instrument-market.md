<p><ul><li>Direct UHT Testing</li><li>Indirect UHT Testing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Direct UHT Testing instruments directly measure the temperature of the product being tested through sensors or probes, providing real-time data on the heat treatment process. Indirect UHT Testing instruments measure the effectiveness of the UHT process by analyzing factors such as microbial growth or chemical changes in the product, without directly measuring the temperature. Both types of instruments are essential for ensuring the quality and safety of UHT processed products in industries such as food and beverage.</p></p>
