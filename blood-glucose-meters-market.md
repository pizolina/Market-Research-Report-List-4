<p><p>Blood Glucose Meters are medical devices used to measure the glucose level in blood. There are two main types - Electrode Type Blood Glucose Meters and Photoelectric Blood Glucose Meters. Electrode Type meters use electrodes to measure glucose levels by detecting the electric current produced by glucose in the blood sample. On the other hand, Photoelectric meters use light to measure glucose levels by analyzing the amount of light absorbed by the blood sample. Different market players offer various types of devices catering to the diverse needs of consumers.</p></p>
