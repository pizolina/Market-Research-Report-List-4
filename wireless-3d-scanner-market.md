<p><p>Wireless 3D scanners are devices that can capture three-dimensional images without being physically connected to a computer or other device. There are two main types of wireless 3D scanners: Laser and Structured Light. Laser scanners use laser beams to measure distances and create a detailed 3D image, while Structured Light scanners use patterns of light to capture depth and create a 3D model. Both types of scanners are used in various industries such as manufacturing, healthcare, and entertainment for tasks like quality control, modeling, and virtual reality applications.</p></p>
