<p><p>Dental fluoride treatments are available in various forms including mouth rinse, varnish, toothpaste, gel, and others. Mouth rinse is a liquid fluoride solution that can be swished around the mouth and then spit out. Varnish is a concentrated fluoride paste that is painted onto the teeth. Toothpaste contains fluoride as an ingredient for regular brushing. Gel is a viscous fluoride treatment that is applied directly to the teeth. Other forms of fluoride treatment may include fluoride supplements or fluoride-releasing dental materials.</p></p>
