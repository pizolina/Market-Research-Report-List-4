<p><p>Low Pressure Extruder Market Types can be categorized into Single-screw Extruder and Twin-screw Extruder. Single-screw extruders are commonly used for processing materials with low viscosity, while twin-screw extruders are preferred for materials with higher viscosity or when a more intense mixing is required. Single-screw extruders are more cost-effective and simpler in design, while twin-screw extruders offer better performance and versatility. Both types of extruders play crucial roles in various industries such as food processing, plastics manufacturing, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
