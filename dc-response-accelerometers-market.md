<p><p>DC Response Accelerometers are categorized into two main types - Capacitive Type and Piezoresistive Type. Capacitive accelerometers measure acceleration by detecting changes in capacitance due to motion, offering high sensitivity and low noise levels. On the other hand, Piezoresistive accelerometers use piezoresistive materials to measure acceleration by detecting changes in resistance, providing high stability and accuracy. Both types are commonly used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and robotics for monitoring and control applications.</p></p>
