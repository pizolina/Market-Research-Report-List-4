<p><p>PCB design tools are categorized into two main types: Basic Type and Professional Type. Professional Type tools are preferred for complex and detailed circuit designs that require precision and accuracy. Both types of PCB design tools cater to different levels of expertise and project requirements in the electronics industry.</p></p>
