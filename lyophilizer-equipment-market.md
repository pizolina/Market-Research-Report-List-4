<p><p>Lyophilizer equipment, also known as freeze dryer, is a device used to remove moisture from biological samples while preserving their structure and integrity. This process involves freezing the material and then reducing the pressure to allow the frozen water to sublimate directly from the solid phase to the gas phase. Lyophilizer equipment is widely used in the pharmaceutical, biotechnology, and food industries for various applications such as drug development, vaccine production, and food preservation.</p><p>The lyophilizer equipment market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years, with a projected CAGR of 10.7% during the forecast period.
