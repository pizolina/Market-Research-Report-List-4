Whey, on the other hand, is a fast-digesting protein that is quickly absorbed by the body, making it great for post-workout recovery. Both Casein and Whey are popular choices for athletes, bodybuilders, and individuals looking to increase their protein intake for muscle growth and recovery.</p></p>
