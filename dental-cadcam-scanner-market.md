<p><p>Dental CADCAM scanners come in various types such as mouth-in scanners, desktop scanners, and other specialized scanners. Mouth-in scanners are handheld devices that are inserted into the patient's mouth for capturing detailed images of the teeth and gums. Desktop scanners are larger machines that sit on a table or countertop and are used to scan dental impressions. Other specialized scanners include portable or chairside scanners that are designed for use in a dental office or clinic setting. Each type of scanner offers unique features and benefits for dental professionals.</p></p>
