<p><p>Wafer Measurement System Market comprises of two main types: E-beam Measurement and Optical Measurement. E-beam Measurement systems use a focused beam of electrons to measure the dimensions and features of a wafer with high precision. On the other hand, Optical Measurement systems use light to capture images, allowing for non-contact measurement of wafers. Both types of systems are essential in the semiconductor industry for ensuring the quality and accuracy of wafer fabrication processes.</p></p>
