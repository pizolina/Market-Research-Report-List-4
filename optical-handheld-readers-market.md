<p><p>Optical handheld readers are devices used to scan and decode data on various types of barcode labels or tags. There are three main types of optical handheld readers in the market: Laser Readers, Linear Imager Readers, and 2D Imager Readers. Laser Readers use laser technology to read barcodes, Linear Imager Readers use a CCD sensor to capture an image of the barcode, and 2D Imager Readers can read both 1D and 2D barcodes using image capture technology. Each type offers unique features and benefits for different types of scanning applications.</p></p>
