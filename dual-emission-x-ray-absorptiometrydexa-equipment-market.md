<p><p>Dual emission X-ray absorptiometry (DEXA) equipment is used to measure bone mineral density in patients. There are two main types of DEXA equipment: Central DEXA bone densitometer and Peripheral DEXA bone densitometer. Central DEXA is used to scan the hip and spine, providing accurate measurements of bone density, while Peripheral DEXA focuses on smaller bones like the wrist, heel, or finger. Both types of equipment are important for diagnosing osteoporosis and monitoring bone health in patients.</p></p>
