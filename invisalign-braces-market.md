<p><p>Invisalign braces market includes three main types: Lingual Braces, Clear Aligners, and Ceramic Braces. Lingual braces are placed on the back of the teeth, making them practically invisible. Clear aligners are transparent and removable, offering a discreet option for straightening teeth. Ceramic braces are similar to traditional metal braces but are made of clear or tooth-colored materials, blending in with the natural color of teeth. Each type caters to different preferences and needs of individuals seeking a more discreet orthodontic treatment option.</p></p>
