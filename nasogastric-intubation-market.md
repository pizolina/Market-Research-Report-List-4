<p><p>Nasogastric intubation market types include Levin Catheter, Salem Sump Catheter, and Dobhoff Tube. Levin Catheter is a single lumen tube used for short-term feeding or medication administration. Salem Sump Catheter is a double lumen tube with one lumen for suction and the other for feeding or medication. Dobhoff Tube is a small-bore feeding tube inserted into the duodenum or jejunum. These different types cater to varying clinical needs and conditions, providing options for healthcare providers to choose the most suitable option for their patients.</p></p>
