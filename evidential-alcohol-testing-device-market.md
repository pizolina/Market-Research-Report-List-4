<p><p>Contact alcohol testing devices require the user to physically touch the device to provide a breath sample for analysis. These devices are typically handheld and portable, making them ideal for on-the-go testing. Contactless alcohol testing devices, on the other hand, use advanced technology such as infrared sensors to analyze breath samples without direct contact. This type of device is convenient for use in high-traffic areas where multiple individuals need to be tested quickly and efficiently.</p></p>
