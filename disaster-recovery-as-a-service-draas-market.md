<p><p>Disaster Recovery-as-a-Service (DRaaS) refers to the delivery of cloud-based or off-site disaster recovery services to help organizations recover their data and applications in the event of a disaster. There are two main market types: Premises-to-Cloud DRaaS, where data is backed up from on-premises infrastructure to the cloud, and Cloud-to-Cloud DRaaS, where data is replicated between cloud services for additional redundancy. Both types offer organizations various options for implementing robust disaster recovery solutions based on their specific needs and infrastructure.</p></p>
