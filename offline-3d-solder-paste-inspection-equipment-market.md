<p><p>Offline 3D Solder Paste Inspection Equipment market includes two main types - Laser-based and PMP-based equipment. Laser-based equipment uses laser technology to accurately measure solder paste volume and height, providing precise inspections. On the other hand, PMP-based equipment utilizes phase-shifting Moiré projection technology for high-speed and accurate 3D inspection of solder paste. Both types offer advanced capabilities for quality control and process optimization in electronics manufacturing, catering to different preferences and requirements of manufacturers.</p></p>
