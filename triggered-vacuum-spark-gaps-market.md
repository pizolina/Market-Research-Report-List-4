<p><p>Triggered Vacuum Spark Gaps are electronic devices used to protect electrical equipment from voltage surges by diverting the excess voltage to the ground. These spark gaps consist of vacuum chambers with two electrodes separated by a small gap. When the voltage reaches a certain threshold level, the gap sparks, providing a low impedance path for the excess voltage to flow.</p><p>The Triggered Vacuum Spark Gaps Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.00% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by the increasing demand for surge protection in industries such as telecommunications, power distribution, and transportation.
