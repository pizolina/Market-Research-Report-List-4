<p><p>Single bore and multi bore samarium filters are two different types of filters used in various industries. Single bore filters have only one channel for fluid to pass through, making them suitable for simpler applications where only one type of filtering is needed. On the other hand, multi bore filters have multiple channels, allowing for different types of filtration to occur simultaneously. Multi bore filters are ideal for more complex applications that require different levels or types of filtration.</p></p>
