<p><p>PET/CT systems are categorized into low-range, middle-range, and high-range slice systems based on the number of detector slices. Low-range slice systems have more than 20 detector slices, providing basic imaging capabilities. Middle-range slice systems have 21-64 detector slices, offering improved image quality and detail. High-range slice systems have below 64 detector slices, offering the highest image resolution and diagnostic accuracy. Each type of system caters to different clinical needs and budget considerations in the healthcare industry.</p></p>
