<p><p>Low voltage busway market can be classified into two main types based on the type of conductor material used: copper conductor and aluminum conductor. Copper conductor busways are known for their high conductivity and durability, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. On the other hand, aluminum conductor busways are more cost-effective and lighter in weight, making them ideal for applications where weight is a concern. Both types of busways are widely used in commercial, industrial, and residential buildings for power distribution.</p></p>
