<p><p>Veterinary anthrax vaccines can be categorized into two main types: Cell Free PA Vaccines and Live Vaccines. These vaccines are made using recombinant DNA technology and are considered to be safe and effective. On the other hand, Live Vaccines contain a weakened form of the anthrax virus, which helps to stimulate a strong immune response in animals. Both types of vaccines are used to protect livestock and pets from anthrax infection.</p></p>
