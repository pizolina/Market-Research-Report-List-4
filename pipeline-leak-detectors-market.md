Pressure Measurement systems work by detecting changes in pressure levels along the pipeline, while Flow Measurement systems monitor the flow rate of the product in the pipeline. Both types of detectors are crucial in ensuring early identification and prevention of leaks, thereby enhancing safety and minimizing potential environmental damage.</p></p>
