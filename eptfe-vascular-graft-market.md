Small caliber grafts are typically used in procedures such as bypass surgery in smaller blood vessels, while large caliber grafts are used in procedures such as aortic aneurysm repair in larger blood vessels. Both types of grafts offer improved patency rates and reduced risk of infection compared to other materials, making them a popular choice in vascular surgery.</p></p>
