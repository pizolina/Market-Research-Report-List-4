<p><p>The commercial vehicle air spring market consists of three main types: convoluted type, sleeve type, and rolling lobe type. Convoluted type air springs are designed with a bellows-like structure for maximum durability and load-carrying capacity. Sleeve type air springs utilize a rubber sleeve to provide a smooth ride and reduce vibration. Rolling lobe type air springs feature a rolling lobe design that allows for a larger deflection and increased flexibility. Each type offers unique benefits for commercial vehicle suspension systems.</p></p>
