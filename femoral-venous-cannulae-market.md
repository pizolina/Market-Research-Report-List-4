<p><p>Femoral Venous Cannulae market offers options in terms of cannula types, such as single-stage and two-stage. Single-stage cannulae are designed for continuous perfusion, while two-stage cannulae allow for dual-stage drainage and infusion. Both types cater to different requirements in cardiac surgery and extracorporeal life support procedures. Single-stage cannulae are commonly used for simple procedures, while two-stage cannulae are preferred for more complex cases where separate venous drainage and reinfusion are needed. Each type of cannula offers distinct benefits based on the specific needs of the patient and surgical procedure.</p></p>
