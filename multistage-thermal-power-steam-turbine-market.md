Condensing turbines are used in power plants where steam is condensed back into water after exiting the turbine, maximizing efficiency. Back pressure turbines are used in industrial applications where steam needs to be at a specific pressure for various processes. The "others" category includes variations like extraction turbines and combined heat and power systems that cater to specific needs in different industries.</p></p>
