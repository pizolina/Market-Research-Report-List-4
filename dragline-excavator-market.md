Diesel-mechanical draglines use a diesel engine to drive a mechanical powertrain, providing the necessary power for excavating. On the other hand, diesel-electric hybrid draglines combine a diesel engine with an electric generator to power electric motors, offering increased efficiency and reduced emissions. Both types of draglines are commonly used in mining and construction applications for their high productivity and reliability.</p></p>
