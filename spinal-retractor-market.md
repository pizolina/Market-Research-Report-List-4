<p><strong>The Spinal Retractor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tubular Retractors</li><li>Blade Retractor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Spinal retractors are devices used in spinal surgeries to hold tissues and organs aside, allowing surgeons better access to the spinal column. There are two main types of spinal retractors: tubular retractors and blade retractors. Tubular retractors consist of a cylindrical sheath and blades that can be adjusted to the desired size. Blade retractors, on the other hand, have sharp edges that are used to hold tissues apart. Both types of retractors play a crucial role in ensuring clear visualization and safe access during spinal procedures.</p></p>
