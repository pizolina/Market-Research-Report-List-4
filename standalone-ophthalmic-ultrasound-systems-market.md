<p><strong>The Standalone Ophthalmic Ultrasound Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>A- Scan</li><li>B- Scan</li><li>Combined Scan</li><li>Pachymeter</li><li>Ultrasound Bio-microscopy (UBM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Standalone Ophthalmic Ultrasound Systems refer to portable devices used for imaging the eye. A-Scan systems use sound waves to measure the length of the eye, while B-Scan systems produce two-dimensional images of the eye's interior. Combined Scan systems offer both A and B scans in one device. Pachymeters are used to measure corneal thickness, while Ultrasound Bio-microscopy (UBM) systems provide high-resolution images of the anterior eye segment. Each type of system plays a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring various eye conditions.</p></p>
