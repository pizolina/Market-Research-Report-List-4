<p><p>Commercial booster pumps are available in two main types - single stage and multiple stages. Single stage pumps are suitable for applications requiring low to moderate pressure, whereas multiple stage pumps are more efficient in applications requiring high pressure. Single stage pumps have a simpler design with only one impeller, while multiple stage pumps have multiple impellers stacked together to increase pressure. Both types cater to different needs in the commercial sector based on the required pressure levels.</p></p>
