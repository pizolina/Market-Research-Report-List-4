<p><ul><li>CSFB</li><li>VOIMS</li><li>Dual Radio/SVLTE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VoLTE ecosystem consists of different market types such as CSFB (Circuit Switched Fall Back), VOIMS (Voice over IMS), and Dual Radio/SVLTE (Single Voice LTE). CSFB allows LTE devices to fall back to 2G/3G networks for voice calls. VOIMS enables voice calls to be made over LTE networks using IMS technology. Dual Radio/SVLTE involves using two radios in a device to simultaneously support LTE data and 2G/3G voice services. Each market type serves a specific purpose in ensuring seamless voice communication over LTE networks.</p></p>
