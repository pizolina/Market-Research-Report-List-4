<p><p>Prebiotic fiber market includes different types such as inulin, mannan-oligosaccharide (MOS), fructo-oligosaccharide (FOS), and galacto-oligosaccharide (GOS). Inulin is commonly found in chicory root and helps promote the growth of beneficial bacteria in the gut. Mannan-oligosaccharides are derived from yeast cell walls and aid in supporting digestive health. Fructo-oligosaccharides are found in foods like bananas and onions, while galacto-oligosaccharides are present in human breast milk and certain dairy products, both promoting gut health and immune function.</p></p>
