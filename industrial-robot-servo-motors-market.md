<p><p>Industrial robot servo motors can be categorized into two main types: AC servo motors and DC servo motors. AC servo motors operate using alternating current electricity, providing precise control and high speed performance. On the other hand, DC servo motors use direct current electricity and are known for their simplicity and ease of control. Both types of motors play a crucial role in industrial robot applications, with AC servo motors being preferred for high-speed applications and DC servo motors for simpler, more cost-effective solutions.</p></p>
