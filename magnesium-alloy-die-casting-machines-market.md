<p><ul><li>Cold Chamber Die Casting Machine</li><li>Hot Chamber Die Casting Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Magnesium alloy die casting machines market consists of two main types: cold chamber die casting machines and hot chamber die casting machines. Cold chamber machines are used for alloys with high melting points such as magnesium, aluminum, and copper. They involve transferring molten metal to the shot chamber before injecting it into the mold. Hot chamber machines, on the other hand, are used for low melting point alloys like zinc and involve keeping the metal in a furnace attached to the machine for immediate injection into the mold.</p></p>
