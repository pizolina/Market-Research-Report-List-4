<p><p>Atrial Fibrillation Treatment Device Market includes two main types of procedures: Catheter Ablation and Maze Surgery. </p><p>Catheter Ablation involves using a catheter to target and destroy the abnormal heart tissue that causes the irregular heart rhythm in patients with atrial fibrillation. </p><p>Maze Surgery is a more invasive procedure that creates a pattern of scar tissue in the heart to disrupt abnormal electrical signals and restore a regular heart rhythm. Both procedures aim to treat atrial fibrillation and improve heart function.</p></p>
