The market includes diffraction grating interferometers, which use a grating to split and recombine light waves for interference analysis. Vertical scanning or coherence probe interferometers provide depth information by scanning the focus across the sample. White light scatter-plate interferometers use a scatter plate to generate interference patterns for analysis. Each type offers unique advantages for different applications in fields such as semiconductor manufacturing, research, and quality control.</p></p>
