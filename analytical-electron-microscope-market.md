<p><p>Analytical Electron Microscopes are advanced tools used in various scientific fields for high-resolution imaging and analysis of materials. There are two main types in the market - Transmission Electron Microscopes (TEM) and Scanning Electron Microscopes (SEM). TEMs utilize a beam of electrons transmitted through a thin sample to create detailed images, while SEMs scan a focused electron beam across a surface to generate 3D images. Both types offer unique capabilities for studying the structure and composition of materials at the nanoscale level.</p></p>
