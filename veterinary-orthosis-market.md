There are two main types of veterinary orthoses based on the location of the limb they target: forelimb and hindlimb orthoses. Forelimb orthoses are specialized devices for the front legs of animals, while hindlimb orthoses are designed for the back legs. These orthoses help improve mobility, reduce pain, and support injured or weakened limbs in animals, allowing them to lead a more comfortable and active lifestyle.</p></p>
