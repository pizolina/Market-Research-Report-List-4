<p><ul><li>SMD Type</li><li>Through Hole Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Specialty resistors market is segmented into SMD type and Through Hole type. SMD type resistors are surface mount devices that are mounted directly onto the surface of a printed circuit board, allowing for more compact and space-efficient designs. Through Hole type resistors, on the other hand, are inserted into holes on the circuit board and soldered in place. Both types have their own advantages and are used in various applications based on factors like power rating, size, and cost.</p></p>
