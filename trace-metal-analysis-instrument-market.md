<p><p>Trace Metal Analysis Instruments market consists of several types, including Atomic Absorption Spectroscopy, X-Ray Fluorescence, ICP-MS, and others. Atomic Absorption Spectroscopy is based on the absorption of light by free metal ions in solution. X-Ray Fluorescence analyzes the elemental composition of a sample by measuring the fluorescent X-rays emitted when a sample is irradiated with X-rays. ICP-MS combines a high-temperature plasma source with mass spectrometry for highly sensitive and precise analysis. Other methods include ICP-OES, ICP-AES, and GF-AAS.</p></p>
