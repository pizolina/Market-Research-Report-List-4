<p><p>Lab-grown diamonds are categorized into two main types: CVD diamonds and HTHP diamonds. CVD diamonds are produced using chemical vapor deposition technology, resulting in high-quality diamonds with minimal impurities. On the other hand, HTHP diamonds are created through high-pressure high-temperature processes, which mimic the natural diamond formation process. Both types of lab-grown diamonds are increasingly popular due to their ethical and sustainable production methods, as well as their comparable quality to natural diamonds at a more affordable price point.</p></p>
