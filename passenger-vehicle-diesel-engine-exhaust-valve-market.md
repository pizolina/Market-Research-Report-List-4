<p><p>Passenger vehicle diesel engine exhaust valves are available in three main market types: Mono Metallic Valve, Bimetallic Valve, and Hollow Valve. Hollow valves have a hollow stem which reduces weight and aids in heat dissipation. Each type of valve caters to different performance and durability needs in diesel engines for passenger vehicles.</p></p>
