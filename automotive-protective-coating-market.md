<p><ul><li>Priming Coating</li><li>Medium Coating</li><li>Surface Coating</li><li>Finishing Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive protective coating market can be categorized into priming coating, medium coating, surface coating, and finishing coating. Priming coating is applied as a base layer to improve adhesion and corrosion resistance. Medium coating provides additional protection against environmental factors. Surface coating enhances the appearance and durability of the vehicle. Finishing coating is a final layer that adds gloss and protection. Each type of coating plays a crucial role in safeguarding the vehicle's exterior and maintaining its aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
