<p><p>Meal fibers market is divided into two main types: soluble fiber and insoluble fiber. Soluble fiber dissolves in water and forms a gel-like substance in the digestive system, helping to lower cholesterol and regulate blood sugar levels. It is found in fruits, oats, and legumes. On the other hand, insoluble fiber does not dissolve in water and adds bulk to the stool, promoting regular bowel movements. It is found in whole grains, nuts, and seeds. Both types of fiber are essential for a healthy diet and digestive system.</p></p>
