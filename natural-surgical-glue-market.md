<p><p>Natural surgical glue market consists of three main types: fibrin glue, collagen glue, and gelatin glue. Fibrin glue is derived from blood plasma and mimics the final stages of the blood-clotting process. Collagen glue is made from animal tissues and is biocompatible, making it suitable for various surgical applications. Gelatin glue is derived from animal collagen and is often used for wound closure in dental and orthopedic surgeries. Each type of natural surgical glue offers unique benefits and applications in the medical field.</p></p>
