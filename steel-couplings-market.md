<p><p>Rigid couplings are used to connect two shafts in a straight line without any flexibility, ideal for applications requiring high precision and stability. Flexible couplings, on the other hand, provide some degree of flexibility to accommodate misalignment and vibration, protecting machinery from damage. Both types of couplings play a crucial role in various industries, with rigid couplings being preferred for precise and high-speed applications, while flexible couplings are chosen for applications with misalignment or vibration issues.</p></p>
