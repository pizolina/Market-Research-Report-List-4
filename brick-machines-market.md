<p><p>Brick machines are available in two main market types: automatic and semi-automatic. Automatic brick machines require minimal human intervention as they are designed to carry out the brick-making process independently. They are equipped with advanced technology to automate tasks such as feeding, mixing, pressing, and stacking. On the other hand, semi-automatic brick machines require some manual intervention during the production process, such as loading raw materials and removing finished bricks. Both types of machines cater to different production scales and requirements in the construction industry.</p></p>
