Metals, such as gold or titanium, are often used for their durability and strength in restorations like crowns and bridges. Polymers, which include materials like composite resins, are frequently used for more cost-effective restorations and can be easily manipulated for various dental procedures. Each type of material offers unique benefits and is chosen based on the specific needs of the patient.</p></p>
