<p><p>Hot pressure cleaners can be categorized into two main types based on their mobility: mobile and stationary. They are ideal for cleaning small to medium-sized areas such as driveways, decks, and patios. Stationary hot pressure cleaners, on the other hand, are larger and more powerful units that are designed for heavy-duty commercial and industrial cleaning tasks. They are typically mounted in a fixed location and are suitable for cleaning larger areas such as factories, warehouses, and parking lots.</p></p>
