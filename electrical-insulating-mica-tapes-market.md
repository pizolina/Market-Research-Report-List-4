<p><p>Electrical insulating mica tapes are widely used in the market with two main types: Mica Glass Tape and Mica Polyester Tape. Mica Glass Tape is a high-performance electrical insulation tape made by combining mica paper with glass cloth, providing excellent flexibility and thermal resistance. On the other hand, Mica Polyester Tape is made by bonding mica paper with polyester film, offering good mechanical strength and electrical insulation properties. Both types of mica tapes are essential for electrical applications that require high temperature resistance and insulation.</p></p>
