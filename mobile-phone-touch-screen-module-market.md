<p><strong>The Mobile Phone Touch Screen Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resistive Type</li><li>Capacitive Type</li><li>SAW (Surface Acoustic Wave) Type</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mobile Phone Touch Screen Module Market includes various types such as Resistive Type, Capacitive Type, SAW (Surface Acoustic Wave) Type, and others. Resistive Type screens are pressure-sensitive and rely on physical pressure to activate touch response. Capacitive Type screens use the electrical properties of the human body to detect touch. SAW Type screens utilize ultrasonic waves to detect touch. Other types may include Infrared Touch Screens, Optical Touch Screens, and Projected Capacitive Touch Screens. Each type has its own advantages and applications in the mobile phone industry.</p></p>
