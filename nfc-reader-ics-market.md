<p><strong>The NFC Reader ICs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full NFC</li><li>ISO/IEC 15693</li><li>ISO/IEC 14443 Type A</li><li>ISO/IEC 14443 Type A+B</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Full NFC reader ICs are capable of reading and writing to all types of NFC tags, including ISO/IEC 14443 Type A and B, as well as ISO/IEC 15693 tags. ISO/IEC 15693 reader ICs are specifically designed to read only ISO/IEC 15693 tags, while ISO/IEC 14443 Type A reader ICs are designed to read only Type A tags. ISO/IEC 14443 Type A+B reader ICs can read both Type A and B tags. Each type of reader IC offers specific capabilities depending on the types of NFC tags being used.</p></p>
