<p><p>Desktop RFID and Barcode Printer market consists of two main types: Smart and Standard. Smart printers have built-in RFID encoding capabilities and can print both RFID and barcode labels in one device, offering increased efficiency and convenience. Standard printers, on the other hand, can only print barcode labels and require a separate RFID encoder for RFID capabilities. Smart printers cater to industries with high RFID usage, while standard printers are more suitable for industries that primarily use barcode labeling.</p></p>
