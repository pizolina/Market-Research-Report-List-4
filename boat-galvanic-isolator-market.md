<p><p>Boat Galvanic Isolators are devices used to prevent corrosion on boats by isolating different metal components to prevent galvanic corrosion. In the market, there are two types of isolators, Signal Isolation, and Power Isolation. Signal Isolation isolates signal paths to prevent interference and maintain signal integrity. Power Isolation isolates power paths to prevent electrical currents from flowing between different metal components, reducing the risk of corrosion. Both types of isolators play a crucial role in protecting boat electronics and structures from damage.</p></p>
